# Stock Track PRO Dashboard - Complete User Guide

## Overview

Welcome to Stock Track PRO! This guide provides a comprehensive breakdown of everything you can do on the dashboard as a new user. The dashboard is designed to help you manage assets, vehicles, team members, and track all activity across your company.

---

## Access Requirements

### Subscription Status
- **Active Subscription Required**: You must have an active subscription (Active or Trial status) to access the dashboard
- **7-Day Free Trial**: New users get a 7-day free trial when signing up
- **Subscription Lockout**: If your subscription expires or becomes inactive, you'll be locked out until you subscribe again

### User Roles
- **Manager**: Full access to all features including subscription management and defect tracking
- **Admin**: Highest level access, can manage multiple companies and delete records
- **User**: Standard access for field work, can check assets in/out and perform inspections

---

The sidebar menu is organised into five main sections:

### 1. Overview

- **Fleet** (`/dashboard/fleet`) - Vehicle management

- **Analytics** (`/dashboard/analytics`) - Detailed reports and charts
- **Activity History** (`/dashboard/history`) - Complete audit trail

- **Team** (`/dashboard/team`) - User management

### 5. Settings
- **Subscription** (`/dashboard/subscription`) - Manage subscription and billing (Manager/Admin only)
- **Companies** (`/dashboard/companies`) - Company management (Admin only)

### üöó Fleet Management

**What You Can Do:**

#### View Vehicles
- See all vehicles in your company's fleet
- View vehicle details: registration, make, model, VIN, mileage, status
- See vehicle images (if uploaded)
- View most recent inspection photos
- Filter by status: Active, Maintenance, Service Due, Retired
- Search by registration, make, model, or VIN

#### Add Vehicles (Manager/Admin)
- Click "Add Vehicle" button
- Enter details:
  - Registration number (required)
  - Make (required)
  - Model (required)
  - VIN (optional)
  - Current mileage (optional)
  - Status (Active, Maintenance, Service Due, Retired)
  - Next service date (optional)
  - Next MOT date (optional)
  - Upload vehicle image (optional)
- **Subscription Limits Apply**: 
  - Starter: Up to 5 vehicles
  - Team: Up to 15 vehicles
  - Business: Up to 40 vehicles
  - Enterprise: Up to 150 vehicles

#### Edit Vehicles (Manager/Admin)
- Click edit icon on any vehicle
- Update any vehicle information
- Change status
- Update service dates
- Replace vehicle image

#### Delete Vehicles (Admin Only)
- Click delete icon (only visible to admins)
- Confirm deletion
- Vehicle and all associated data will be removed

#### Perform Vehicle Inspections
- Click on a vehicle to view details
- Complete inspection form:
  - Record mileage
  - Take photos (multiple angles: front, driver side, passenger side, rear, interior)
  - Mark defects if found
  - Add notes
- Inspection history is automatically saved
- Defects automatically trigger notifications to managers

**Export Options:**
- Export vehicle list to CSV/Excel
- Include all vehicle details and status information

## Security & Data Protection

### Company Data Isolation
- Each company's data is completely isolated
- Users can only see data from their own company
- Even admins are restricted to their company's data (unless managing multiple companies)

### Role-Based Access Control
- Different permission levels for different roles
- Managers and Admins have full access
- Users have limited access for field work
- Secure authentication via Firebase

### Audit Trail
- Complete history of all actions
- Track who did what and when
- Exportable for compliance and reporting
- Immutable record of changes

## Getting Started Checklist

As a new user, here's what you should do first:

1. **Complete Onboarding**
   - Sign up and create your company
   - Select your trial plan (Starter, Team, Business, or Enterprise)
   - You'll get a 7-day free trial

2. **Set Up Your Fleet**
   - Go to Fleet page
   - Add your vehicles (up to your plan limit)
   - Upload vehicle images
   - Set service dates

3. **Add Your Assets**
   - Go to Assets page
   - Add tools and equipment
   - Generate QR codes for each asset
   - Print QR codes and attach to assets

4. **Set Up Locations**
   - Go to Locations page
   - Add your sites/locations
   - Organise assets by location

5. **Invite Your Team**
   - Go to Access Codes page
   - Generate access codes
   - Share codes with team members
   - They can join your company using the codes

6. **Perform Your First Inspection**
   - Go to Fleet page
   - Click on a vehicle
   - Complete inspection form
   - Take photos and record mileage

7. **Explore Analytics**
   - Go to Analytics page
   - View your metrics and charts
   - Export reports
   - Set up date range filters

8. **Manage Subscription**
   - Go to Subscription page
   - Review your current plan
   - Upgrade if needed
   - Set up billing portal access

## Tips & Best Practices

### Asset Management
- Always generate QR codes for new assets
- Keep asset images up to date
- Regularly update asset status (maintenance, broken, etc.)
- Use locations to organise assets by site

### Fleet Management
- Perform regular vehicle inspections
- Update mileage after each inspection
- Set service dates and MOT reminders
- Mark defects immediately when found
- Resolve defects promptly to keep vehicles active

### Team Management
- Use access codes to invite new team members
- Assign appropriate roles (User, Manager, Admin)
- Regularly review team member access
- Remove inactive users

### Reporting
- Export data regularly for backup
- Use date range filters for specific periods
- Print reports for meetings and presentations
- Monitor fleet health score regularly
